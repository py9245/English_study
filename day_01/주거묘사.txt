I live in Ansan with my girlfriend.
We live in a villa, not an apartment. It has two rooms.
Behind our house, there is a small mountain. It is very quiet and nice.
Also, there is a stream called Ansancheon. The water flows slowly, and many people walk there.

When you enter the front door, you can see the living room and kitchen together.
On the left side, there is a bathroom.
Next to the bathroom, there are two rooms. One is big, and one is small.
The big room is our bedroom. The small room is for clothes and other things.

The house is clean and comfortable.
It is not too cold in winter, so the gas bill is not high.
I think this house is great. It is simple, but very good for us.
I like living here.
